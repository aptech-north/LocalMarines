About LOCALS MARINE

Discovering Ocean Wonders, Inspiring Conservation

Our Story

LOCALS MARINE began as a small marine conservation project in 2010.

What started as a humble initiative has grown into Pakistan's premier marine education center.

Today, we house more than 200 species of marine life across 50+ exhibits.

View Gallery Visit Us

Our Mission

To inspire ocean conservation through education, research, and unforgettable experiences.

  • Educate students annually
  • Rescue marine animals
  • Promote sustainable practices

Our Vision

To become South Asia's leading center for marine conservation.
